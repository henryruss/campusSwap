How Campus Swap Works

  • Browse Listings Explore a wide range of items posted by fellow students. From textbooks to tech, find great deals on products you need, right on campus.
  • Request Items Can't find what you're looking for? Ask anonymously by posting a "Seeking" listing. Let other students know what you're after and get notified when they post it.
  • Sell Items Turn your unused items into cash! Simply upload a photo, description, and price to list your item. Buyers can send an offer, or pay instantly with your "buy-now" price.
  • Grow Your Brand Whether you're selling personal items or promoting a small business, showcase your products on your seller page. Build credibility with customer reviews, updates, and more to attract more buyers.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Campus Swap is a marketplace platform designed for college students to buy, sell, store, and stow goods in an efficient and cost-effective manner.
  • Campus Swap is open to all verified college students and faculty members with a valid school email.
  • Campus Swap's verification and seller rating system ensures safety and trust among users. However, users should always use their best judgement.
